This mod pulls all of its sounds from sources licensed under Creative Commons and other similarly permissive licenses. A list of attributions for these recordings, and their associated licenses, can be found below. All files were edited from their originals, generally to reduce length and background noises, and are converted to mono audio where some originals are stereo.
